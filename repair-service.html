Common Issues and Solutions

üíª Computer Won't Turn On

Try these steps:

  1. Check if the power cable is properly connected
  2. Try a different power outlet
  3. Check if the power button on the PSU is switched on
  4. Listen for any beeping sounds when starting
  5. Remove and reseat the RAM modules

If these steps don't help, your power supply or motherboard might need professional inspection.

üîä Strange Noises

Identify the noise:

  • Clicking: Possible hard drive failure
  • Grinding: Fan bearing issues
  • Buzzing: Loose component or cable
  • Whining: Power supply or GPU stress

Unusual noises should be checked immediately to prevent damage!
